The Problem

Many households buy more food than they can consume. Food gets forgotten in the fridge, expires, and ends up in the bin. This contributes to unnecessary greenhouse gas emissions and wastes money.

Leftover Buddy helps households manage food smarter and reduce waste through intelligent reminders and recipe suggestions.

Our Solution

Smart Expiry Tracking

Track your food items and receive reminders before they expire.

AI Recipe Suggestions

Get personalised recipes based on ingredients you already have.

Community Sharing

Share excess food with nearby neighbours to avoid waste.

How It Works

1. Add Ingredients

Upload or scan your food items when you buy them.

2. Get Smart Alerts

Receive reminders before food expires.

3. Cook or Share

Use recipe ideas or share leftovers with your community.
